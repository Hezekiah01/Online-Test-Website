About the Scholarship

The Covenant University Talent Transformation Scholarship is a visionary program to nurture creativity, innovation, and leadership in the next generation of tech professionals.

Participants are divided into three categories:

  • Junior: Ages 7–12
  • Senior: Ages 13–15
  • Professional: Post high school
